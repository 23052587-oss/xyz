# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include using welcoming language and being respectful of differing viewpoints.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
The project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers will follow these community guidelines to determine consequences for any actions they deem unacceptable.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Violations will be reviewed and investigated with a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.0.
